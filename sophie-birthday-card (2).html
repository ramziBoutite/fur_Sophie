Love Letter · 愛の手紙

From Ramzi, With All His Heart

Dear Sophie,

Happy Birthday, my love. Twenty-four years of you in this world — and I am endlessly grateful that two of those years have been mine to share with you. I know I am not perfect. There are moments I say the wrong thing, a lot happened during this time, moments I act without thinking, moments I let you down in ways I am not proud of. I am sorry — deeply, genuinely sorry — for every one of those moments. You deserve someone who always gets it right, and I promise you, I am working and training every single day to become that person. You are worth every effort I can give, Sophie. Every single one. The distance between us was definitely not easy on me, on both of us. Some nights I feel like I am desperately needing for you — your voice, your presence, just you (and your PS2 games XD). But every morning I wake up knowing you exist on the other side of it, and that is enough to keep going. Giving up on us is not something I am capable of. You are it for me. Only you I chose to continue my life with, and I believe we can overcome literally any obstacle that lays ahead of us. This may sound too silly of me to write, but I want you to know — two years of memories with you, and I already know I want a lifetime more. I want to grow old beside you and look back at all of this — the distance, the hard days, the waiting — and smile knowing every second of it was worth it, looking back at everything we achieved together. Breaking up is not an option. There is nothing between us that we cannot overcome together. Until fate finally closes this distance, I carry you with me. Always.

Yours, only yours — Ramzi
